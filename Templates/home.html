AGRICULTURE

Agriculture is the art and science of cultivating the soil, growing crops and raising livestock. It includes the preparation of plant and animal products for people to use and their distribution to markets. Agriculture provides most of the world's food and fabrics.Wikipedia
Based on the rapid cimate change in Ghana's environment, the farmers incur a lot of cost in their daily cultivation. Agriculture is one of the most productive sectors in our country and taking care of it will bring more income and reduce cost on the farmers.
